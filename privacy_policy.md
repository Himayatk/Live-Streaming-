# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 7, 2026  
**Last Updated:** January 7, 2026

Live Streaming Inc. ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Live Streaming mobile application (the "App" or "Service").

---

## 1. Personal Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

**Account Registration:**
When you sign up for Live Streaming using social media login (Google, Facebook, Apple), we collect:

- Nickname/Display Name
- Social Media Account ID
- Email Address
- Profile Picture

When you sign up with email, we collect:

- Email Address
- Password (encrypted)
- Nickname/Display Name

**Platform Connections:**
When you connect third-party streaming platforms (YouTube, Facebook, Twitch, Instagram, etc.), we collect:

- Platform account tokens (OAuth access tokens)
- Channel/Page IDs
- Platform-specific identifiers
- Public profile information from connected platforms

**Payment Information (for Premium subscriptions):**

- Subscription type and duration
- Payment date and time
- Receipt information
- Payment status
- Billing email address

**Customer Support:**

- Email address for correspondence
- Support ticket details and chat history
- Device and app diagnostics shared voluntarily

**Events and Promotions:**

- Email address for event participation
- Shipping information for prize delivery (if applicable)

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Usage Data:**

- App usage statistics (features used, frequency, duration)
- Stream history (platforms streamed to, duration, timestamps)
- Settings and configurations
- In-app navigation patterns

**Device Information:**

- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- App version
- Device language and region settings
- Unique device identifiers
- IP address
- Network connection type

**Stream Performance Data:**

- Video resolution and bitrate settings
- Audio quality settings
- Stream health metrics (dropped frames, connection stability)
- Platform-specific streaming metadata

### 1.3 Temporary Processing Data (Not Stored)

**Camera and Microphone:**
During live streaming, your camera feed and microphone audio are processed in real-time to transmit to streaming platforms. This data:

- Is NOT stored on our servers
- Passes through our service only for streaming purposes
- Is transmitted directly to your chosen streaming platform(s)

**Facial and Body Data (for Future VTuber/Avatar Features):**

- Facial expressions and movements for avatar animation
- Body tracking data for motion capture
- This data is processed locally on your device and is NOT stored
