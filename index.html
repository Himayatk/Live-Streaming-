The easiest way to delete your account and all associated data is directly through the Live Streaming app:

  1. Open the Live Streaming app on your device
  2. Tap on Settings (gear icon in the top-right corner)
  3. Scroll down and tap Account Settings
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Review the information about what will be deleted
  6. Confirm deletion by tapping Delete My Account Permanently
  7. You may be asked to enter your password or verify via email for security

üìÑ Data We May Retain

For legal compliance and security purposes, we may retain limited information:

  • Fraud Prevention: One-way encrypted identifiers may be kept for 6 months to prevent fraudulent account re-creation
  • Anonymized Analytics: Aggregated, non-identifiable usage statistics that cannot be traced back to you
