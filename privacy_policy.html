Live Streaming Inc.

Effective Date: January 7, 2026

Live Streaming Inc. ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Live Streaming mobile application (the "App" or "Service").

1. Personal Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

Account Registration: When you sign up using social media (Google, Facebook, Apple), we collect your Nickname, Social Media Account ID, Email Address, and Profile Picture. If you sign up with email, we collect your Email Address, Encrypted Password, and Nickname.

Platform Connections: When you connect streaming platforms (YouTube, Facebook, Twitch, etc.), we collect OAuth access tokens, Channel IDs, and platform-specific public profile info.

Payment Information: For premium subscriptions, we collect subscription records, payment receipts, and billing email addresses. (We do not store full credit card numbers).

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: App statistics, stream history, settings, and navigation patterns.
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, IP address, unique identifiers, and network type.
  • Stream Performance: Resolution settings, bitrate, and stream health metrics.

1.3 Temporary Processing Data (Not Stored)

Camera and Microphone: During live streaming, your A/V feed is processed in real-time to transmit to platforms. This data is NOT stored on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the streaming service.
  • Manage your account and authentication.
  • Process payments and subscriptions.
  • Communicate with you regarding service updates or support.
  • Improve app performance and develop new features.
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud.

3. How We Share Your Information

We DO NOT sell your personal information.

Information Sharing Scenarios:

  • Streaming Platforms: We share necessary tokens and metadata with platforms like YouTube and Twitch to enable your streams.
  • Service Providers: We use trusted third parties for cloud hosting (NAVER Cloud), payment processing (Google/Apple), and analytics.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose info if required by law or to protect rights and safety.

4. Third-Party Services

By connecting your accounts, you agree to the privacy policies of:

  • YouTube: Terms of Service | Google Privacy Policy
  • Facebook: Privacy Policy
  • Twitch: Privacy Notice

Revoking Access

You can revoke permissions at any time via your Google Account Permissions or Facebook/Twitch settings.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data as long as your account is active. If you request deletion:

  • Personal info is deleted within 30 days.
  • Backups are purged within 90 days.
  • Transaction records may be kept for 5 years for tax compliance.

To delete your account, go to Settings > Account > Delete Account in the app.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access a copy of your data.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for processing.

Contact us at privacy@livestreaming.app to exercise these rights.

7. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect info from children under 13. If discovered, such data will be deleted immediately.
